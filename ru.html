About Us

The Design

BlackStone is unique to it's time.
Built in Detroit this unique coffee shop brings
Coffee, Street Art, and Plants all into one place
The insparation for BlackStone was simple, we wanted a place for everyone.
By utilizing local artists talent, We are able to create a truly unique experance.
Inspired by local street artists and with constantly rotating art done by local artists and local coffee roasters
We where able to create BlackStone.



The Beans

We Use Brazillian Coffee Beans
There are many different beans from Brazil,
But for us
The Acabica Bean is best, it is known for being low in acidity.
This Blend Delivers:
A Distinct Flavor
A Smooth Body
Sweet Flavor
With hints of Chocolate and Caramel
and a slight nut flavor
Our Coffee pairs well with pastries,
Which is why we carry a varity of those too.
